    Lateral Prevertebral Muscles

    Muscle Origin Insertion Innervation Actions
    Rectus capitis lateralis Transverse process of the atlas (C1). Jugular process of the occipital bone. Branches from the loop between C1 and C2. Stabilizes and assists flexion of the atlanto-occipital joint.
    Middle Scalene Posterior tubercles of transverse processes of C3–C7.
  • Superior surface of the 1st rib (posterior to groove for subclavian artery)
    		• Anterior rami of C3–C7 of spinal nerve.
  • Elevates the 1st rib during forced inspiration
  • Flexion of the neck via bilateral contraction
  • Laterally flexes neck unilaterally contraction
    		•
    Posterior Scalene Posterior tubercles of transverse processes of C5–C7. External border of the 2nd rib. Anterior rami of C7–C8.
  • Elevates the 2nd rib during forced inspiration
  • Flexion of the neck via bilateral contraction
  • Laterally flexes neck unilaterally contraction
    		•
